quatre quarts

Please note: bells may not (yet) ring reliably when running Quatre quarts in a background tab. If you want to use it in the background, it's best you open it in a separate window, otherwise your browser might stop it from running after a while.

about

Quatre quarts is a timer for time blindness.
Its aim is to emphasize how much is left from the whole duration, rather than its abstract time value.
